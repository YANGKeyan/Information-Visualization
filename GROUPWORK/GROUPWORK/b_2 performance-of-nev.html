2. New Energy Vehicle Technology

2.2 Performance of New Energy Vehicles

New energy vehicles come in various types, each with its unique features and advantages.

Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs): BEVs are fully electric, relying solely on batteries for power. They offer excellent efficiency and low operating costs.

Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs): PHEVs combine electric and gasoline power with ability to charge battery, providing flexibility for longer trips.

Hybrid Electric Vehicles: Hybrid electric vehicles use a combination of electric and gasoline power to enhance fuel efficiency and reduce emissions.
